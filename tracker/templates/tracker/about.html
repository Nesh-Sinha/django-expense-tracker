About This App

Expense Tracker is a simple and efficient web application built to help you manage your daily expenses and stay within your budget. Whether you're a student, a working professional, or someone trying to build better money habits, this tool provides a clear and organized way to track your spending.

✨ Features:

  • Add, edit, and delete income and expense entries
  • Set a monthly budget and get alerts when you're close to exceeding it
  • Visualize your spending with interactive charts
  • Export your data to CSV or PDF for offline tracking
  • Fully responsive and mobile-friendly interface

🔒 No Signup Required

This app uses session storage to temporarily store your data during your visit. You can use it freely without creating an account. Just open the app and start managing your expenses!

👨‍💻 Built With:

  • Django (Backend)
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Frontend)
  • Chart.js (for data visualization)
  • Bootstrap (for UI styling)

💡 Why This App?

Managing money shouldn’t be complicated. This app was created to offer a lightweight solution for anyone looking to gain better control over their finances without the need to sign up or share personal data.
